Rough Diamond Studio

A modular studio for audio-first systems.

Born from real need: as a novice podcaster, I needed tools that didn't exist. So I built them. Rough Diamond Studio started as a dashboard for myself, then evolved into modular systems for the greater market. From podcast intake to content libraries, show notes, and digital products — all under one studio.
